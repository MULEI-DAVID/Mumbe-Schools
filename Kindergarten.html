Our Kindergarten Philosophy

At Mumbe Kindergarten, we believe that the early years are the most critical period for development. Our nurturing environment provides a strong foundation for lifelong learning through play-based activities that stimulate curiosity and imagination.

We embrace the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) approach, focusing on developing key competencies such as communication, collaboration, critical thinking, and creativity.

Our dedicated teachers create a warm, caring atmosphere where children aged 3-6 feel safe to explore, ask questions, and develop at their own pace.

Age-Appropriate Learning

Tailored programs for each age group: Baby Class (3-4), Middle Class (4-5), and Pre-Unit (5-6)

Holistic Development

Focus on cognitive, social, emotional, physical, and creative growth

Creative Expression

Art, music, dance, and dramatic play to foster imagination

Nature Connection

Outdoor learning in our secure playground and garden

Our Learning Programs

Language & Literacy

Developing communication skills through storytelling, phonics, and early reading activities. Our language-rich environment encourages vocabulary building and expression.

  • Phonics and word recognition
  • Storytelling and role play
  • Pre-writing skills development

Numeracy Skills

Fun, hands-on activities to develop early math concepts. Children learn through play with counting games, pattern recognition, and shape exploration.

  • Counting and number recognition
  • Shape and pattern exploration
  • Measurement and sorting activities

Creative Arts

Encouraging self-expression through various art forms. Children explore different materials, colors, textures, and develop their imagination.

  • Painting, drawing, and sculpting
  • Music and movement activities
  • Dramatic play and puppetry

Our Kindergarten Facilities

Our purpose-built kindergarten wing features:

  • Bright, spacious classrooms with age-appropriate furniture
  • Dedicated nap/rest area with individual cots
  • Indoor play area for rainy days
  • Child-sized bathrooms with low sinks and toilets
  • Secure outdoor playground with safety surfaces
  • Sand and water play stations
  • Child-friendly garden for nature exploration
  • Art studio with various creative materials

All facilities are designed with child safety as our top priority, featuring rounded corners, non-toxic materials, and secure entry systems.

OUR STORY

20 Years of Excellence in Education

A WORD FROM OUR DIRECTOR

Mr. Muteti, Founder & Director

"At Mumbe Group of Schools, our journey began with a simple dream — to provide quality, values-driven education in the heart of Makindu. Over the years, we have grown from humble beginnings into a community of learners, leaders, and changemakers."

"As we look to the future, our mission remains clear: to nurture excellence, inspire purpose, and empower every child to reach their full potential. Together with our dedicated staff and supportive parents, we are building not just a school — but a legacy."

20 Years of Impact and Excellence

Established in 2005, Mumbe Group of Schools has proudly served the Makindu community for over two decades, growing from a single classroom to a respected educational institution known for academic excellence, moral grounding, and holistic development.

Our Journey at a Glance

2005

Founded with a vision to provide accessible, quality education rooted in African values and Christian principles

2008

First cohort sits for KCPE with impressive results, setting the tone for academic success.

2010

Expansion to include junior secondary classes due to increasing demand.

2013

Introduction of co-curricular programs: music, drama, sports, and scouting.

2015

Construction of modern science and ICT labs to support STEM education.

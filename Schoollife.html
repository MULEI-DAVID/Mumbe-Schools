Student Life at Mumbe

Our Vibrant Community

At Mumbe Group of Schools, we believe education extends far beyond the classroom. Our vibrant school community provides students with countless opportunities to grow, explore, and develop lifelong skills.

From kindergarten to high school, students are encouraged to discover their passions, develop leadership skills, and build meaningful relationships with peers and mentors.

Our diverse student body comes together to create a supportive environment where every student feels valued and empowered to reach their full potential.

House System

All students belong to one of our four houses, fostering camaraderie and healthy competition through academic and extracurricular activities.

Leadership

Opportunities for student leadership through prefects, club presidents, and class representatives.

Mentorship

Older students mentor younger ones, creating a supportive community across grade levels.

Community Service

Regular community outreach programs teach empathy and social responsibility.

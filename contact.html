Frequently Asked Questions

Admission requirements vary by school level. Generally, we require a completed application form, previous school reports, a birth certificate, and passport photos. For specific requirements for each school, please visit the school's page or contact our admissions office.

Our fee structure varies depending on the school and grade level. Fees cover tuition, textbooks, and most learning materials. Additional costs may include uniforms, meals, and extracurricular activities. For a detailed fee schedule, please contact our finance office or request information through our contact form.

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid for qualifying students. Scholarship opportunities are available for exceptional academic, artistic, or athletic talent. Financial aid is awarded based on demonstrated financial need. Please contact our admissions office for application details and deadlines.

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (football, basketball, athletics, swimming), performing arts (drama, music, choir), STEM clubs (robotics, coding), debate club, environmental club, and more. Activities vary by school level and season. Visit our School Life section for more details.

To schedule a campus tour, please call our admissions office at +254 712 345 678 during office hours or fill out the contact form on this page. We recommend scheduling tours at least 48 hours in advance. Tours are typically conducted on weekdays between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM.

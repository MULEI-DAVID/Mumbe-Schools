Our Junior Secondary Program

Mumbe Junior Secondary School provides a transformative educational experience for students in Grades 7-9, serving as a critical bridge between primary and senior secondary education.

We fully implement Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), focusing on developing essential skills and competencies through practical, real-world applications.

Our program emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills to prepare students for senior secondary pathways and future careers.

Critical Thinking

Developing analytical skills and independent thought processes

Digital Literacy

Advanced technology skills for the modern world

Collaboration

Team-based projects and cooperative learning

Career Pathways Exploration

STEM Pathway

Focuses on Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. Prepares students for careers in engineering, medicine, computer science, and research.

  • Advanced mathematics
  • Physics and chemistry labs
  • Robotics and coding
  • Engineering principles

Social Sciences Pathway

Focuses on human society and social relationships. Prepares students for careers in law, education, psychology, and public service.

  • History and geography
  • Psychology and sociology
  • Political science
  • Community service

Arts & Creativity Pathway

Focuses on creative expression and performance. Prepares students for careers in design, media, performing arts, and literature.

  • Visual and performing arts
  • Creative writing
  • Media production
  • Design technology

Business & Entrepreneurship

Focuses on commerce, finance, and enterprise. Prepares students for careers in business, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship.

  • Business studies
  • Financial literacy
  • Marketing principles
  • Entrepreneurship projects

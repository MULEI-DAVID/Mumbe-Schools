Our Primary School Program

At Mumbe Primary School, we provide a comprehensive education for students from Grade 1 to Grade 6. Our program is designed to nurture intellectual curiosity, develop critical thinking skills, and foster a love for learning.

We follow the Kenyan Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), focusing on developing key competencies such as communication, collaboration, critical thinking, creativity, and digital literacy.

Our experienced teachers create a supportive environment where students are challenged to reach their full potential academically, socially, and emotionally.

Academic Excellence

Consistently high performance in national examinations and academic competitions

Holistic Development

Focus on academic, social, emotional, physical, and creative growth

Digital Literacy

Technology-integrated learning with modern computer labs and resources

Reading Culture

Well-stocked library and reading programs to foster a love for books

Our Learning Approach

CBC Curriculum

Our teaching aligns with Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum, focusing on developing skills and competencies through practical, real-world applications.

  • Practical learning experiences
  • Continuous assessment
  • Personalized learning paths

STEM Education

Special emphasis on Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics through hands-on projects and experiments.

  • Science lab experiments
  • Coding and robotics clubs
  • Math Olympiad training

Creative Arts

Developing artistic expression through music, drama, visual arts, and crafts.

  • Art and craft workshops
  • Music instrument training
  • Annual drama festivals

Core Subjects

Languages

English, Kiswahili, and French language development focusing on reading, writing, speaking, and comprehension skills.

Mathematics

Building strong numeracy skills through practical applications and problem-solving approaches.

Science & Technology

Hands-on exploration of physical, biological, and environmental sciences with technology integration.

Social Studies

Understanding communities, cultures, history, geography, and civic responsibilities.

Creative Arts

Exploring various art forms including music, drama, drawing, painting, and crafts.

Physical Education

Developing physical fitness, sports skills, and healthy lifestyle habits.

Primary School Facilities

Our Learning Environment

Our purpose-built primary school facilities include:

  • Modern, well-equipped classrooms with smart boards
  • Science laboratory for practical experiments
  • Computer lab with internet access
  • Comprehensive library with reading spaces
  • Art and music studios
  • Multi-purpose hall for assemblies and events
  • Sports fields for football, athletics, and netball
  • Secure playground for younger students
  • Dining hall serving nutritious meals

Excellence in Girls' Education

Established in 2008, Mumbe Girls High School has become a beacon of excellence in girls' education in Makueni County. We provide a nurturing environment where young women develop intellectually, emotionally, and socially to become leaders of tomorrow.

Our holistic approach combines rigorous academics with character development, leadership training, and empowerment programs designed specifically for young women.

With a focus on STEM education, creative arts, and leadership development, we prepare our students for university education and successful careers in diverse fields.

Academic Excellence

Consistently ranked among top girls' schools in the county with a 95% university placement rate

STEM Focus

State-of-the-art science labs and robotics programs to foster innovation

Leadership

Strong alumnae network providing mentorship and career guidance

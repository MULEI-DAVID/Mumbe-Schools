Welcome to Mumbe Schools

Our History

Founded in 2002, Mumbe Group of Schools was established with a vision to provide holistic, accessible, and quality education to children and youth in Makindu and the surrounding areas of Makueni County.

What began as a modest kindergarten with just two classrooms and a handful of dedicated teachers has now grown into a reputable educational institution, serving hundreds of learners across all levels of basic education.

Today, the Mumbe Group of Schools stands as a beacon of hope, transformation, and opportunity in the region. The group is committed to continuous growth, innovation in teaching and learning, and the holistic development of every learner—spiritually, intellectually, and socially.

Our Mission

Empower learners to achieve excellence in both academic and extra-curricular domains.

Our Vision

To cultivate excellence that reflects both national and international standards, and to raise a reliable, principled, and capable child.

Why Choose Mumbe Schools?

At Mumbe Group of Schools, we believe that every child deserves a strong foundation, a nurturing environment, and the opportunity to excel. With over two decades of experience in delivering quality education from kindergarten to high school, we are proud to be one of the leading educational institutions in Makueni, Kenya.

Holistic Education

We go beyond academics to nurture students intellectually, emotionally, morally, and socially—preparing them to thrive in a dynamic world.

Experienced and Caring Staff

Our qualified teachers and support staff are passionate about teaching and committed to the success and well-being of every learner.

CBC-Ready and Future-Focused

Our junior high and other programs are fully aligned with Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), offering hands-on, skills-based learning that prepares students for real-life challenges.

Safe, Inclusive Learning Environment

Whether it's our girls' or boys' high schools, or our early childhood classes, every campus is designed to promote safety, discipline, and respect.

Proven Academic Excellence

Our students consistently perform well in national exams, and many alumni have earned scholarships and gained admission to top universities and colleges.

Strong Values and Leadership Development

We instill values such as integrity, responsibility, and leadership to develop confident, respectful, and community-minded individuals.
